GAMEMODES

NORMAL MODE

Normal Mode is the normal campaign in th difficulty intended by the developers. No adjustments made outside of what the developers originally intended are made.

HARD CORE

Hard Core is a gamemode where more advanced parkour and advanced enemies appear earlier than normal. Playing this feels like if Dark Souls was played at 10X speed.

KILL RUN

Kill Run is a time trial of 5 levels, depending on how quickly you finish the level you will be rewarded with a medal. Obtaining 'Master Rank'(the highest medal) on all the levels will unlock the 'Finishing Line' Katana and Gloves. In Kill Run you can collect clocks which will increase the time you have, killing enemies will also increase time.
